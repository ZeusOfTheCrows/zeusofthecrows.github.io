Favourites

    most people who know me assume it's tea, which is reasonable, but i'd drink many more bloody maries if

    2. they weren't alcoholic: whilst i do like alcohol, sometimes it's not really appropriate

  • favourite greek letter: Υ υ

    most letters in the greek alphabet have zero or one direct descendants in latin; upsilon has five:
